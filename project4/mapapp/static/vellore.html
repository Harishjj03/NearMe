VELLORE FORT PARK

Vellore Fort Park is a serene and well-maintained public space located around the historic Vellore Fort in Tamil Nadu. The park offers a peaceful escape from the city's hustle and is popular among locals and tourists for walking, jogging, and family outings. Surrounded by the majestic stone walls of the 16th-century Vellore Fort, the park blends natural beauty with rich history. Lush greenery, flowering plants, shaded pathways, and seating areas make it an ideal spot for relaxation and recreation. The presence of a moat around the fort
